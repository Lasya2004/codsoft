Project

Daily care

Many people face the challenge of caring for elderly loved one who needs help with daily activities. Daily care is a great solution that provides professional and sympathetic care for your parent in the comfort and familiarity of their own home.Daily care is a device that reminds users to take medications. This device contains electric clocks involved, which can be preset for certain times of the day to set off an alarm. More sophisticated medicine reminders can also detect when they have been opened, and therefore when the user is away during the time, they were supposed to take their medication, they will be reminded of it through call and message. The device will remind in the form of a light and buzzer. Device is a portable one, the person can carry it when he leaves the home. It contains many slots based on the requirement. For example: if a person is supposed to take multiple medicines in one sessions with different time slots he can arrange the medicines accordingly and can consume without any confusion. This can be helpful to all kind of people irrespective of the age.
